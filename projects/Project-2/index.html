What is 3D Printing?

"3D printing is a fun hobby and useful to the future. It is a printer that melts plastic and places it mathematically to build something new from a digital model."


Accomplishments of 3D Printing:

"3D printing has come a long way from where it started. It started out as printing small models like cubes and then even little toy boats. Now it is being used to print houses, prosthetics, candy, firearms, casts, and musical instruments. Anything you can imagine and make a 3d model of you can print!"


What Kind of Printer Should I Get?

"It is hard to know what printer you should get. After I got my first printer here is what I learned. First thing you should look for in a 3D printer is auto leveling. This will prevent many man made errors. If you are printing long or larger things a treadmill printer would be best for you. If not you can get non-treadmill 3D Printer."
