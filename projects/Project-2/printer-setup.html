A Step-by-Step Guide To 3D Printing

Home Materials Needed Printer Setup Steps To Print

Here is how you will want to set up your printer!
  1. "First you will want to assemble it as it says to in instructions."

  2. "Second you will want to find an area to print. Preferably on a table of some sort and where it will not annoy you as some can be noisy."

  3. "Third You will want to clean your printing bed with alcohol"

  4. "Fourth you will want to insert the plastic filament into the nozzle after mounting the filament wheel on a post on the printer."

  5. "Fifth is you want to make sure the bed is level. If manual leveling make micro-adjustments until level. Test with a leveling print that normally comes with printer."

  6. "Finally load your sd card with a model, insert it into the printer and print"
