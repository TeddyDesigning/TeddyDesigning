Step #2 Testing that your bed is leveled!

"If you have an auto leveling bed have the printer level the bed for you. If you have a manual leveling printing bed you will want to make sure it is level. To do this you can use 2 small levels on the vertical and horizontal side of the printing bed. Then turn the gears in the bottom 4 corners of the printing bed until level. Turning it left lifts it up and right lowers the corner. Once it is level, lower the nozzle to the bed. There needs to be a small gap between the nozzle and the bed. To test this use a playing card and slide it between the nozzle and the bed. You want to feel very light friction when moving the card under the nozzle."
