Step #1 Set up your 3D printer!

"The first thing you will want to do is assemble your 3D printer following the instructions that you got with your printer. You will want to assemble it somewhere where it will not get messed with and will not annoy you if it is noisy. After you have assembled the printer where you want, clean the printing bed with alcohol. Turn it on and make sure it works by manually controlling how the nozzle moves and the temperature. If it does not respond to your commands, check if everything was assembled correctly or bring to someone to check it for you."
