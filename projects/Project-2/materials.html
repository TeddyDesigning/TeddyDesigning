Here are the materials you will need!
  1. "The first and obvious thing you will need is a 3D printer."

  2. "The second thing you will need is a computer that can read an sd card and load 3D models onto the sd card."

  3. "The third thing you will need is 3D printing filament (make sure it is the right size for your printer)."

  4. "The fourth thing you will need is plastic cutters."

  5. "The fifth thing you will need is a bonding tool to attach 3D printed pieces together(if you have to print something in pieces)."

  6. "The sixth thing you would want is some rubbing alcohol to clean your 3D printer bed."
